The "Why": Causes of Back Acne in Females
Back acne occurs when your body's oil glands (sebaceous glands) go into overdrive. In women, this is primary driven by hormonal fluctuations, sweat trapped by sports bras, and the "shower runoff" effect where hair conditioner residue clogs pores.
