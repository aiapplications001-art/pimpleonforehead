How to Choose Your Perfect Formula

Very Oily / Sweaty

Stick to pure gels. Pond’s, Minimalist B5, or Sebamed are your safest bets.

Damaged Barrier

If your skin is peeling from Retinol, you need Ceramides. Bioderma or CeraVe are essential.

The 3-Step Verification Checklist

  • 1. Decode the Ingredients: Avoid Isopropyl Myristate and Coconut derivatives.
  • 2. "Recommended" vs "Tested": Look for active endorsements, not just trial participation.
  • 3. The Humidity Patch Test: Test for 48 hours during seasonal shifts (Monsoon/Summer).

The Paradox

Skipping moisturizer causes skin to overproduce oil to compensate for dehydration. This traps you in a cycle of shine and breakouts.

Top Ingredients to Seek

  • Hyaluronic Acid: Weightless moisture
  • Ceramides: Barrier repair
  • Niacinamide: Oil control
  • Panthenol: Soothing
